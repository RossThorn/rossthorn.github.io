About Me

Creativity and logic may often be seen as opposite ends of the same spectrum: the typical “left-brain” vs. “right-brain” argument. An individual may be seen as a creative or logical, but people have a hard time combining those adjectives to describe a single person or idea.

I believe cartography sees its full potential at the nexus of all that is artistic and expressive and all that is reasonable and rational. Science and art can be beautifully portrayed together in maps.

I enjoy mapping things that may not have been mapped before. I value originality and creativity, attempting to make every map I make stand out from others. This reflects my ideals as a person, in which I try to be myself and not anyone else.

I am currently a master’s student at the University of Wisconsin - Madison, residing in the best state of all time. I am a musician and a Magic: The Gathering player. Contact me if you’re interested in maps, music, or Magic!
