NOME

Nome is an app designed to help you find the best place for you to live when looking at locations in a new city! While this project is still in development (so I can't give too many details on how it works), it is easily one of the projects I'm most proud of. I designed the functionality of the app, the logo and all branding, as well as most of the current functionality in the prototype. However, it's been a labor of love for a few friends and me to brainstorm logistics and other potential functionality.
