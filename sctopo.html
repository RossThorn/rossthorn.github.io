Sugar Camp Topo Map

Previous Next

A an early mapping project of mine of a location that is near and dear to my heart. This is a topographic map of my hometown, designed in the style of USGS quad maps. Although the maps themselves function more as tools than art, I believe that contour maps are inherently visually attractive. I created a few versions of this map for each member of my immediate family, placing locations that were uniquely important to each person. Some aspects and elements of the map are crude, but I think this map is too important to me to leave out.
