For Your Convenience

with Ross and Jimmy

What is this?

In this new age, there are several important areas in life that need to be critically assessed to make a solid opinion. The problem is that there are so many topics to have an opinion on. Yet, a functioning member of society is expected to take a stance on everything. How can you sift through all the information there is and develop a meaningful stance? That's where we come in.

For Your Convenience (FYC) with Ross and Jimmy is a show where we cover the topics you should probably have a solid opinion about. You know that the news is already misinforming you and the internet is too vast and cluttered with junk to search for every good credible source. FYC wants to make that easier on you, so you can become a well-informed individual in today's world.

Who are we?

For Your Convenience is hosted by Ross Thorn, an aspiring cartographer and musician, and Jimmy Pecquex, a musically gifted, tree-loving, lepidopterophobe. We are two former college roommates that love to laugh, play music, and partake in tabletop nerdiness once in a while.

Essentially, we are two well-rounded nerds that hope to bring you quality ratings and rankings across several important areas of life. We hope that you will listen to our podcast and laugh along with us.
